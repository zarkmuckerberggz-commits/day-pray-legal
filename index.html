📖 Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7th July 2026

Who We Are

Day Pray is a faith-based prayer app designed to help users pray more consistently. Contact: zarkmuckerberggz@gmail.com

What Data We Collect

  • Your first name (entered during onboarding)
  • Your selected objectives and commitment level
  • Journal entries you write within the app
  • Feedback messages you choose to send us
  • Your prayer streak and session history
  • Verses, reflections, and prayers you save to Favourites
  • Your preferred notification time (if enabled)
  • Your trial start date

We do not collect payment information, location data, or advertising IDs.

How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely in Firebase (Google) cloud servers. This means your streak, journal, and favourites are preserved even if you reinstall the app or switch devices.

Feedback messages are sent via EmailJS to our email inbox.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Day Pray experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

AI-Generated Content

Day Pray uses Groq to generate personalised reflections and prayers. Only your selected emotion/need and the corresponding verse are sent to this service. Your name, journal entries, and personal data are never shared.

Children's Privacy

Day Pray is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your Rights

Under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) of Malaysia, you may access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Deletion requests will be processed within 30 days. Contact us at zarkmuckerberggz@gmail.com.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — data storage
  • EmailJS — feedback transmission
  • PostHog — analytics
  • Groq — AI reflection generation
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google Play — subscription billing

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance.
